Biography

Hey whats up everyone. My name is Peeta Mellark. I'm from District 12. I enjoy baking, working out, and spending time with my wife, Katniss.

Years ago, I participated in the Hunger Games, where I was crowned the 74th winner, along with my wife. After that, I participated in the 75th Hunger Games, as well as fought for the Revolutionaries in the following Civil War.

Hunger Games

Many years ago, I participated in the Hunger Games not once, but twice. By using my artistic skills from being a baker, as well as my strength from lifting heavy bags of flour each day, I was able to survive both ordeals. Each time, I had been close to death, but through Katniss's help and sheer willpower, I was able to survive.

Civil War

In the Civil war, I had fought with the Revolutionaries. However, because I was traumatized from the Hunger Games, as well as torchered by the Capital, I was not able to help out as much as I had wished to. After the war, I served a seat in the Government Senate for a bit, before living a quiet life on the outcrops of Panem.

Life at Lehigh

I came to Lehigh to further my education, and use it to improve my baking process and machinery in the future.
